The threat landscape

African financial institutions are under attack

Fintechs lose revenue to reconciliation friction and cross-border settlement delays. Banks lose hundreds of millions of CFA to coordinated database breaches that unfold in a single night. Both problems have the same root cause: centralized systems with single points of failure. KalyRails is the fix for both.

For fintechs

Cross-border remittance fees eat margins. Manual reconciliation eats back-office capacity. Regulators demand transparency your database can't prove. KalyRails L2 gives you instant settlement, automatic reconciliation, and a regulator-ready audit trail — without your users ever touching crypto.

For banks

Database breaches inherit all the access controls you trusted. Velocity limits enforced in application code can be bypassed. Cross-branch anomalies get spotted hours after the losses post. KalyRails Bank moves those rules into smart contracts that even a root attacker cannot forge.
